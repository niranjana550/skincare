A simple skincare routine includes three essential steps: cleansing, moisturizing, and protecting. Start by washing your face with a gentle cleanser to remove dirt and oil without stripping the skin. Follow up with a moisturizer suited to your skin type to keep it hydrated and maintain its natural barrier. Finally, apply a broad-spectrum sunscreen with at least SPF 30 during the day to protect your skin from harmful UV rays and prevent premature aging. Stick to this routine daily, and for added care, you can exfoliate 1-2 times a week to remove dead skin cells and boost skin radiance.
